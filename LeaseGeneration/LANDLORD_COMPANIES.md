## MCP Agent Enrichment

When the MCP Enrichment Agent processes leases with these landlords, it can search the web to find:

- **Company Type**: REIT, Private Equity, Public, etc.
- **Stock Ticker**: For publicly traded companies
- **Market Cap**: Current market capitalization
- **Total Assets**: Assets under management
- **Credit Rating**: From S&P, Moody's, or Fitch
- **Financial Metrics**: Revenue, NOI, debt-to-equity ratio
- **Portfolio Details**: Property count, square footage, property types
- **Geographic Focus**: Primary markets
- **Financial Health Score**: Computed risk assessment
- **Recent News Sentiment**: Current market perception

## Tenant Companies

Tenants are selected from the S&P 500 companies list, providing realistic Fortune 500 and large-cap companies across all major industry sectors.
